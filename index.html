Complete Calculation Theory & Reference

1 Trace Resistance

$$R_{dc} = \rho_{cu} \frac{L_{total}}{W_{tr} \times T_{cu}}$$

Defines the baseline attenuation before AC skin-depth characteristics take effect.

  • \(\rho_{cu}\): Copper Resistivity (\(1.68 \times 10^{-8} \, \Omega{\cdot}m\))
  • \(L_{total}\): Geometric Trace length in meters
  • \(W_{tr}, T_{cu}\): Trace Width and Copper Thickness (1oz \(= 35{\mu}m\))

2 Modified Wheeler Formula

$$L = \frac{\mu_{0} N^{2} d_{avg} c_{1}}{2} \Big[ \ln(\frac{c_{2}}{\rho}) + c_{3}\rho + c_{4}\rho^{2} \Big]$$

An industry standard empirical formulation for square/rectangular planar spiraled inductors.

  • \(\mu_{0}\): Vacuum permeability (\(4\pi \cdot 10^{-7}\))
  • \(\rho\): Fill Factor \((D_{out} - D_{in}) / (D_{out} + D_{in})\)
  • \(d_{avg}\): Average Diameter \((L_{avg} \cdot W_{avg})\)
  • \(C_1=1.27, C_2=2.07, C_3=0.18, C_4=0.13\)

Ref: S. Mohan et al., "Simple Accurate Expressions for Planar Spiral Inductances" (IEEE 1999).

3 Quality Factor & Diagnostics

$$Q = \frac{X_{L}}{R} \implies \frac{2 \pi f L}{R}$$

Dictates the resonance efficiency and FDX operational bandwidth envelope.

  • \(f\): Target Operating Frequency (134.2 kHz)
  • Target \(L\) for MRD2: 47.0 µH
  • Target \(Q\) for MRD2: 10 to 20

Ref: Texas Instruments RI-STU-MRD2 Target Specifications.
